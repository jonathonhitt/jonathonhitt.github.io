Services

Our primary services are CCTV installations, Television and Audio mounting, and cable installation and management.  If you have a more specific problem, please send us a message or call with the details, if we cannot provide the service ourselves we'll reach out to some of our trusted partners.

CCTV

There are so many types of cameras that it can be frustrating, time consuming, and most likely end up with a product that does not do what you need it too. 

Our equipment and installation is going to beat any store bought system.
 

Listed below are some of the best features our systems offer:

 

  • Onsite Recoding
  • Remote Viewing
  • No monthly fees EVER
  • Strong design and materials
  • Recording continues when internet connection is broken
  • Higher resolutions and more options when building a kit with us

Home Media

We have mounted displays for an array of customers in both commercial and residential environments. No television is to big for us to mount for you. 

Our equipment and installation is going to be high end and all mounts we carry are full motion meaning you get full control of how your display will look.
 

Why should you pick us?

 

  • We mount all sizes
  • We can mount your display to virtually any service
  • We will hide your wires, essentially making the television appear to float on the wall
  • Whether you are streaming only, using an antenna, or subscribed to cable we can initialize your service

Data

Are you changing offices, and need certain lines activated to your phone system or network? We can do that. Maybe your tired of your kids complaining about lagging in their online games. We can ensure premium wireless coverage as well.
When we rewire a home or office, we ensure that when we leave you will have the coverage you needed. Many of our customers are surprised when they see how much they've saved on their ISP bill.
 

Why go with Eagle-i on running my lines?
 

  • We listen to your needs and based on that we use combined years of education and experience to give you exactly what you are looking for
  • No matter the scale of your set up we have ways to get you connected. Trying to get broadband to your guest house? We offer point 2 point solutions to avoid the high cost of a burial cable
